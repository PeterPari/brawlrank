How BrawlRank Calculates Rankings

BrawlRank aggregates tier lists from 9 independent sources including data platforms, pro players, content creators, and community votes. Each source rates brawlers from S (best) to F (worst). Numerical scores are assigned (S=6, A=5, B=4, C=3, D=2, F=1), source-specific weights are applied, and a weighted average is calculated. Data sources receive the highest weights because they measure actual player performance rather than subjective opinion.
