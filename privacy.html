Privacy Policy

Clear disclosure for cookies, advertising, and data use on BrawlRank.

This policy explains what information BrawlRank may collect, why it is collected, how Google AdSense and analytics tools may use cookies, and how to contact us about privacy-related questions.

Last updated: March 29, 2026

1. Introduction

BrawlRank is an independent Brawl Stars tier list and meta reference. This privacy policy applies to the BrawlRank website and explains how information is handled when you browse the site, view rankings, interact with advertising, or contact the team about support or privacy matters.

The site is maintained by Tech-savvies on behalf of BrawlRank. BrawlRank is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Supercell.

2. Information We Collect

BrawlRank is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Depending on how the site is configured and used, the following categories of information may be collected:

  • Basic analytics information such as page views, device type, browser type, approximate location, and on-site interactions.
  • Cookie and local storage data used to remember preferences, measure traffic, or support advertising and consent choices.
  • Contact details you choose to provide when sending a privacy or support request by email or through an external contact form.
  • Advertising-related data made available by third-party ad partners, including Google AdSense, to measure performance and limit fraud.

We use this information to operate the website, understand performance, improve the accuracy and presentation of BrawlRank content, respond to requests, and support lawful advertising.

3. Third-Party Advertising

BrawlRank uses cookies and web beacons for advertising and measurement. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.

Google AdSense may use the DoubleClick cookie and related advertising technologies to serve interest-based ads, measure campaign performance, and help prevent invalid activity.

Third parties, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

You can learn more about how Google handles information in advertising services by reviewing Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies, Web Beacons, and Analytics

BrawlRank and its service providers may use cookies, local storage, tags, and web beacons to understand how visitors use the site, maintain basic functionality, remember consent choices, and evaluate the effectiveness of advertising or analytics tools.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Blocking some cookies may affect how certain parts of the site work, including advertising and preference storage.

5. How To Opt Out of Personalized Ads

You may opt out of personalized advertising from Google by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also review broader information about Google's practices and controls in Google's Privacy Policy.

If you are located in a region with additional privacy rights, you may also contact us directly to ask questions about data handling or request assistance related to data you have provided voluntarily.

6. Children's Privacy

BrawlRank is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the site, contact us so the information can be reviewed and, if appropriate, removed.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in site features, advertising integrations, analytics tooling, or legal requirements. When material changes are made, the date at the top of this page will be updated.

8. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or requests, email info@tech-savvies.com with the subject line “BrawlRank Privacy Request.” You can also use the contact information listed on the Contact page.

Quick Summary

BrawlRank may use analytics, cookies, and Google AdSense advertising technologies to operate the site, understand traffic, and serve ads. You can control personalized ads through Google and contact the team directly for privacy questions.
