01 — Advertising

Google AdSense

BrawlRank uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google and its advertising partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other websites.

To learn more about how Google uses data when you use BrawlRank, visit google.com/policies/privacy/partners.

You can opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings, the Digital Advertising Alliance, or the EDAA opt-out tool (EU).

02 — Analytics

Microsoft Clarity

BrawlRank uses Microsoft Clarity for session analytics — heatmaps, session recordings, and aggregated usage data — to understand how visitors use the site.

Clarity loads only after you accept the analytics consent banner. No data is collected before consent. Dismiss the banner and no analytics cookies are set.

For details on how Microsoft handles this data, see the Microsoft Privacy Statement.

03 — Cookies

Cookies

BrawlRank sets no first-party cookies and stores no personal data on its own servers. Two third-party services may set cookies:

  • Google AdSense — advertising cookies set by Google and its partners. Present regardless of the analytics consent banner.
  • Microsoft Clarity — session analytics cookies, set only after you accept the consent banner.

You can manage or delete cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect ad relevance.

04 — Data collected

Data we collect

BrawlRank requires no account, collects no personal information, and runs no forms that submit data to BrawlRank servers. All tier list data comes from publicly available third-party rankings and statistics.

Any data collection that occurs is performed by Google AdSense and Microsoft Clarity under their own privacy policies.

05 — EU consent

EU user consent

BrawlRank complies with Google's EU User Consent Policy. The analytics consent banner lets EU/EEA users opt in or out of Clarity analytics before any cookies are placed. Google AdSense operates under its own consent mechanisms.

06 — Children's privacy

COPPA

BrawlRank is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has submitted personal information through this site, contact us and we'll remove it.

07 — Third-party links

External sites

BrawlRank links to YouTube, Noff.gg, MmonsteR, Driffle, BrawlTime.ninja, and other external sites. Each has its own privacy policy. BrawlRank is not responsible for their data practices.

08 — Policy changes

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of the page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of BrawlRank after changes constitutes acceptance.
